Project-Based Work (Add-Ons)

Some projects require additional investment due to complexity, scope, or one-time build effort. These are scoped as separate, fixed-fee projects with clear expectations.

  • You receive a written estimate before any project work begins.
  • 50% of the fee is required upfront, with the remaining 50% due on delivery.
  • No hidden fees or surprise invoices — everything is agreed upon in advance.

Examples of add-on projects: complex multi-step automations, custom dashboards, app prototypes, data integrations, and larger workflow transformations.
