About PeakOps Automation

We help busy professionals and teams design better workflows, eliminate manual work, and build automation solutions that scale without burnout.

Our Philosophy

Most teams are wasting hours every week on work that could be automated or streamlined. Not because they're inefficient — but because no one has time to step back and fix the workflow.

That's where PeakOps comes in. We specialize in finding those opportunities, designing better processes, and building the small automations that quietly save hours every month. We focus on what actually works in the real world — not perfect solutions that never get used.

What We Do

Process Improvement

Custom Automation

From Excel macros to Python scripts to dashboards, we build solutions that fit into your existing stack and actually get used.

Productivity Engineering

We think like engineers about your operations. Small, repeatable systems that reduce friction and let your team focus on high-value work.
